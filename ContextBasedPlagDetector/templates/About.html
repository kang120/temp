About this checker...

It is a type of plagiarism checker which detect the context-based plagiarism. In this checker, Natural Language Processing techniques and Machine Learning were being used. Documents or texts that are considered suspicious can be put in this checker. Comparison between the texts and documents will be done. Thus, the result of plagiarism will be shown as well.
